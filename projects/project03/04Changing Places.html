Changing Places - Tord Gustavsen Trio

2023.5

I am a rumbling carriage on the road,

a wanderer in the jungle.

When the boat pass around the lighthouse

towards the pier,

the pier drew close ——

When the stranger rides away,

the girl in his carriage,

knows that this afternoon

would still repeat itself thousands of times.

Staring at me with your eyes,

your eyes full of the real and unreal,

The jungle repeats its dance,

of ceasing back and looming close ——

your eyes melt into the crannies of the woods,

turning into a leaf.

Gray-haired mother,

she lives on top of the lighthouse.

With trembling hands,

by an oil lamp,

she sews in and out.

Across the ocean,

a cicada bursts into cries.

In a boat next to the shore,

the princess is waiting –

a vagrant living in her heart,

longing and praying.

Stranger still strides in the woods,

but in those leaves,

she is nowhere to be found.

When night comes,

the lighthouse above lengthened the princess’s shadow,

her eyes look lonely and full of riddle.

Waves after waves against the reef,

the foamy sea in constant flux,

just like her hemline in the past.

Many years past,

when stranger rides through the woods again,

all the leaves suddenly become her eyes.

On every cliff,

at the top of the lighthouse,

a silhouette elongates against the oil lamp.

Against the sound by the waves,

everything is like yesterday,

just the old sun and stars no longer by.
